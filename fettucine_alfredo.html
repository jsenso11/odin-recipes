Fettucine Alfredo

Description

This is an image of Fettucine Alfredo.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt/Pepper/Garlic Salt seasoning
  • Romano and Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Throw pasta in boiling water for 8 minutes 30 seconds
  2. Melt butter and cream together on the stove, seasoning and stirring in the cheeses.
  3. Toss pasta in cheese sauce.
